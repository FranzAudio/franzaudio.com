About

I've spent over a decade at Babbel working at the intersection of product, technology, and language learning. As a product leader, I focus on turning strategic vision into tangible experiences through lean methodologies, fast iteration, and a relentless focus on the learner.

I thrive on structural impact: I executed the **Product Discovery pilot** at Babbel, establishing the company’s current validation standards. I believe in growing people as much as products—having **mentored 5 Product Managers** to help them master their craft and reach their next level of seniority.

My leadership style is grounded in the wisdom of industry pioneers. I’ve had the privilege of being mentored by **Jeff Patton** and **Petra Wille**, whose philosophies on outcome-centric discovery and product leadership continue to shape my approach to building strong, autonomous teams.

Before product, there was music. For years I worked as a guitarist, singer, composer, and producer — five years in Barcelona, then Berlin — collaborating with international artists, releasing records, and running live sound for festivals across Europe.

Music is the second language everybody understands.

That conviction still shapes how I think about product: it's not about features, it's about what people feel. This deep connection between communication and craft is what led me from a technical support role to leading product innovation at scale.
