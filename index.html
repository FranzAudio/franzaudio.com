About

I've spent over a decade at Babbel working at the intersection of product, technology, and language learning. I believe deeply in Babbel's mission: creating mutual understanding through language.

Language is more than vocabulary and grammar — it's connection, empathy, and a bridge between people and cultures. As a product leader, I focus on turning bold ideas into tangible experiences through lean methodologies, fast iteration, and a relentless focus on the learner.

When I speak Italian, I'm a bit of a different Franz than when I speak English, Spanish, or German. That personal relationship with language is what drives my work every day.

Before product, there was music. For years I worked as a guitarist, singer, composer, and producer — five years in Barcelona, then Berlin — collaborating with international artists, releasing records, and running live sound for festivals across Europe.

Music is the second language everybody understands.

That conviction still shapes how I think about product: it's not about features, it's about what people feel. It's also where this domain got its name.
