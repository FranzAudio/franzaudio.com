# ConvoPro / Babbel Speak – Product Portfolio Summary

## Overview

ConvoPro (later rebranded as *Babbel Speak*) is an AI-powered conversational learning experience designed to help language learners practice speaking in a safe, adaptive, and engaging environment.

It addresses a structural gap in language learning: users consume content but struggle to actively produce language, especially in speaking scenarios.

---

## Product Impact (Key Signals)

The initiative has already demonstrated strong early traction and behavioral impact:

- ~35% of users exposed to ConvoPro return to the feature within the first week  
- AI Conversations drive significantly higher **speaking engagement** vs. scripted experiences  
- Users produce **meaningfully more words per session** compared to legacy speaking formats  
- Sessions combine speaking + listening, increasing **time spent per interaction**

## Core Product Vision

The long-term vision is to make **speaking the primary modality of learning**, shifting Babbel from:

> “learning about a language” → “actively using a language”

---

## Key Product Concepts

### 1. Speak Tab (Product Surface)

A dedicated tab launched on iOS and Android to centralize all speaking experiences.

Impact:
- Increases discoverability of speaking features
- Positions speaking as a core, not secondary, activity

---

### 2. Welcome Scenarios (Onboarding)

Problem:
Users hesitate to start speaking due to fear, lack of confidence, and cognitive overload.

Solution:
- Guided first conversation
- L1 support at the start
- Progressive transition into L2
- Early personalization signals

Expected impact:
- Increase first-session completion rate  
- Increase **words spoken in first session**  
- Improve D1–D7 return to Speak  

[Insert funnel: entry → first utterance → conversation completion]

---

### 3. AI Conversations (Core Loop)

Core loop:
1. Enter scenario  
2. Speak with AI  
3. Receive feedback  
4. Continue interaction  

Key performance insight:
- While legacy Guided Conversations show higher **completion rates**,  
  ConvoPro drives higher **active speaking output**, which is the stronger long-term retention driver.

---

### 4. Lesson → Speak Connection

Experiment:
Expose users to speaking immediately after completing a lesson.

Hypothesis:
- Reinforces learning through immediate usage  
- Increases cross-feature engagement  

Expected impact:
- ↑ Return to Speak (D1–D7)  
- ↑ Return to Learn (D1–D7)  

[Insert Amplitude cohort: users exposed vs not exposed]

---

### 5. UX Harmonization

Scope:
- Replay audio  
- Slow playback  
- Translation  

Impact:
- Reduces friction for beginners  
- Improves comprehension and control  
- Increases session completion  

Constraint:
Different tech stacks → focus on **UX consistency, not backend unification**

---

### 6. Enriched Feedback (Upcoming)

Goal:
Move from binary feedback to actionable guidance.

Planned features:
- Missed word detection  
- Inline corrections  
- Error explanation  
- Pronunciation feedback  

Principle:
- Deterministic systems where possible → reduce cost and increase reliability

## Key Learnings

### Speaking Drives Retention
Users who engage in speaking:
- Return more frequently  
- Build stronger habits  
- Show higher long-term engagement  

---

### AI > Scripted (for Depth)
- Scripted → better completion  
- AI → deeper engagement  

→ Strategic bet is on AI

---

### Beginner Friction is Critical
Small UX improvements (e.g. slower audio, replay) have outsized impact on:
- Completion  
- Confidence  
- Retention  

---

### Personalization Increases Stickiness
Users respond strongly when the system:
- Adapts to their level  
- Reflects their interests  
- Feels responsive  

---

## Strategic Direction

### Short Term
- Improve onboarding (Welcome Scenarios)  
- Reduce beginner friction  
- Increase session completion  

---

### Mid Term
- Integrate into Seamless Learning Path (SLP)  
- Expand feedback quality  
- Strengthen lesson → speak loop  

---

### Long Term
**Speak First paradigm**

- Speaking becomes the primary entry point  
- Lessons become supporting tools  

---

## Risks & Tradeoffs

### Cost vs Engagement
- AI conversations increase cost per session  
- Requires clear ROI via retention and engagement  

---

### UX Complexity
- Too much feedback → cognitive overload  
- Too little feedback → low perceived value  

---

### Product Cannibalization
- Risk of shifting usage away from lessons  
- Requires careful orchestration across surfaces  

---

## Positioning Statement

> ConvoPro transforms language learning from passive consumption into active communication by making speaking accessible, safe, and habitual from day one.
